Machete

The machete in *Far Cry 1* is a melee weapon used by the protagonist, Jack Carver, for close-quarters combat. It is a versatile, lightweight blade effective for silent takedowns and cutting through foliage. Available early in the game, it is ideal for stealthy approaches and quick strikes. The machete's design reflects its practical use in the game's tropical island setting. Despite its simplicity, it remains a reliable tool throughout the campaign.

Status

Type: Melee Weapon

Damage: Moderate (effective in close combat)

Range: Short (melee attacks only)

Attack Speed: Fast

Weight: Light

Availability: Common (found from the start of the game)

Primary Use: Stealth takedowns, clearing vegetation, close combat

Ammunition: Does not require ammunition
